About This Journal

Hi, I'm Hugh Boone.

I've been a person who never could (wanted to?) fit in. I've tried to make work that I enjoyed and hoped that people would come along for the ride if they liked it. Now, I want to make a rukus. It'll make for an interesting life.

So, this blog was rebooted in 2021. After 20 years of writing I decided to start over, to start a new me. I really don't know where it's going to go, but you are welcome to join me and see. If you don't like what you see, that's OK. It's not for you, no shame in that.
